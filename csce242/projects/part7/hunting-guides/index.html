Forest Hunting

Venture deep into dense forests with expert guidance from Blair, Joey, Duane, and Kyler. With decades of combined experience, these seasoned hunters know the best trails, feeding grounds, and hidden spots to find trophy game. Blair specializes in tracking whitetail deer, using advanced scent control and stand positioning techniques. Joey is a master of bow hunting, teaching precise shot placement and ethical harvesting. Duane focuses on predator hunting, offering lessons on calling in coyotes and bobcats with expert accuracy. Kyler rounds out the team with his extensive knowledge of survival tactics, ensuring every hunter is prepared for the unpredictable wilderness. Whether you're after big game or small game, these guides provide an immersive forest hunting experience.

Mountain Hunting

Take on high-altitude hunting challenges with Cole Kramer, a renowned mountain hunting guide with over 15 years of experience. Specializing in extreme terrain hunts, Cole has guided hunters through the rugged peaks of Alaska and the Rocky Mountains. He is an expert in tracking elusive species like mountain goats, bighorn sheep, and elk in high elevations. His deep understanding of wind patterns, elevation shifts, and animal behavior ensures that every hunter gets the best shot opportunity possible. With Cole’s guidance, you'll master glassing techniques, long-range shooting, and endurance training required for mountain hunting success. If you're looking for an adrenaline-filled, physically demanding hunt with breathtaking scenery, Cole is the guide you want by your side.

Desert Hunting

Experience the unique challenges of desert hunting with an elite team of guides who know the arid landscapes better than anyone. Leading the way are veteran hunters Jake “Dust Devil” Morrison, Travis “Sand Stalker” Reed, Lucas “Cactus Jack” Bennett, and Elijah “Sharp Eye” Carter. Jake has spent over two decades tracking mule deer and pronghorn antelope in some of the harshest desert terrains. Travis is a desert predator specialist, helping hunters call in elusive coyotes, bobcats, and mountain lions using expert-level tactics. Lucas focuses on desert bighorn sheep hunting, teaching strategies to navigate rocky canyons and vast plateaus to locate these majestic animals. Elijah is the team’s long-range shooting expert, ensuring hunters make precise, ethical shots even in windy desert conditions. With their combined experience, this team provides an unforgettable desert hunting adventure for any skill level.
